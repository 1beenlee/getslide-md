# Prompt: evaluate one generated deck

~~~txt
INPUTS: source.md, benchmark.json, DECK_BRIEF.md, index.html, and docs/EVALUATION_RUBRIC.md.

Evaluate the deck against the rubric. For each dimension, give a 0–4 score and concise evidence. Cite relevant source wording in your reasoning, but do not copy long passages. List omitted source facts, unsupported claims, assumption-transparency findings, narrative findings, readability/overflow risks, critical failures, and an overall pass/fail recommendation.

Do not reward decorative polish over source fidelity. Do not claim that a string match proves factual accuracy. Treat configured benchmark terms as review cues, not the whole truth.
~~~
